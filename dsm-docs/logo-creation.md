# Logo Creation

## Tool
Recraft AI (https://www.recraft.ai)
Project: https://www.recraft.ai/project/b5dc2fd1-5eb7-424a-a444-3c273c5e229a

## Iteration 1
**Prompt:** Flat minimalist logo icon of a round cookie with a triangular bite taken out of the right side. Simple clean edges, warm golden-brown color, small chocolate chip dots. Solid white background. Vector style, no gradients, no shadows, no text. Designed to be recognizable at 32x32 pixels.

**Result:** A circle with a triangular bite looked like Pac-Man. Not the intended direction.

## Iteration 2
**Feedback:** A circle with a triangular bite looks like Pac-Man. Preferred something looking like a robot that has taken a bite of a cookie.

**Prompt:** Flat minimalist logo of a friendly small robot holding a cookie with a bite taken out of it. The robot has a simple rounded square head with two dot eyes and a small antenna. Cute and approachable. Clean vector style, limited color palette (blue-gray robot, golden-brown cookie), solid white background. No text, no shadows, no gradients. Designed to work as a logo at small sizes.

**Result:** Design adopted initially.

## Licensing Issue (2026-03-09)

**Problem:** Recraft AI's ownership FAQ (https://www.recraft.ai/blog/ownership-and-commercial-use-faq) clarifies that free tier images created after 2024-08-12 have no commercial use rights and remain Recraft's property. The logo was generated on the free tier after that date.

**Options considered:**
1. Mechanical transformation (flip, recolor, pixelate) -- Rejected. Does not create a new work; underlying design remains Recraft's.
2. Recreate concept with a tool that grants commercial rights on free tier -- **Selected.**
3. Pay for Recraft subscription -- Rejected. Unnecessary when free alternatives exist.

**Decision:** Recreate the logo using Ideogram (ideogram.ai), which grants commercial rights with no ownership claim on free tier. The robot-with-cookie concept is original; only the specific Recraft rendering is restricted. Reuse the iteration 2 prompt on Ideogram to generate a new image.

**Status:** Resolved.
